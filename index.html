A look @ my tech

MacBook Pro 2015

The 2015 MacBook Pro is arguably Apple's best MacBook Pro. This Mac has many features that new Macbooks either downgradded or eliminated altogether.

LG V20

The LG V20 is a flagship LG phone that was first produced in 2016. I picked this phone up because it includes a powerful DAC and audio amplifier, a removable battery, and impressive technical specs.

TimBuk 2 Messenger Bag

This is my messenger bag. I've had it for a while now. It fits everything I need with plenty of room to spare. I would recommend it to anyone looking for a modern, high-quality bag.

KZ Earbuds

I purchased these earbuds from Amazon for about $15, and for the price they have excellent sound quality. These make great "beater" earbuds.

Pilot G2 Pens

For the price I don't think that you can get better pens. The writing performance is always smooth and consistent. You can find these in an assortment of colors.

A Small Notebook

Lastly, I always carry a small notebook with me for when inspiration strikes.
